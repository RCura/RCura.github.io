Robin Cura
PhD in Geography and GIScience

Current position

Post-Doctoral researcher at UMR Géographie-cités and Deezer Research Team, within the RECORDS project.

Bio

Robin Cura is a Doctor in Geography, with a PhD thesis focused on quantitative geography, agent-based modelling and spatial data visualization. With a geographical information engineering scholarship, he’s interested in the development and dissemination of quantitative methods for geographic data science and to the use of exploratory and visual spatual-data analysis.

Education

Université Paris 1 Panthéon-Sorbonne | Paris, France

  • Ph.D. in Geography within the Géographie-cités research lab | 2013 - 2020
    Thesis (in french) : “Interdisciplinary modelling of settlement systems through the collective : co-construction and visual analysis of a simulation model”

  • Master Carthagéo : Geography, GIScience and Cartography | 2003 - 2005
